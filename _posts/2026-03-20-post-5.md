This week, I attended the 9th edition of the Julia hackathon. The attendees were the usual suspects from Mainz and Zürich, with a couple of fresh faces from elsewhere as well. I mostly worked on integrating [JustPIC.jl](https://github.com/JuliaGeodynamics/JustPIC.jl) into my own code [DEDLoc](https://github.com/ArneSpang/DEDLoc) and rethinking the way I compute numerical errors. Click [here](https://gpu4geo.org/) to find out more about the biannual hackathon.
